üìù Long Content for Chunk Testing

This section contains longer content to test chunk splitting and navigation. The TTS extension should split this into manageable chunks based on your settings.

Each chunk should be a reasonable size for comfortable listening. You can adjust the chunk size in the Settings tab to make chunks larger or smaller according to your preference.

The navigation controls allow you to skip forward or backward through the chunks, giving you full control over what you want to listen to. This is especially useful for long articles where you might want to skip certain sections.

You can also use the keyboard shortcuts Alt+Left and Alt+Right to navigate between chunks, or Space to pause and resume playback. These shortcuts work even when the popup is closed.

The content analysis feature shows you exactly how many chunks the text has been split into and which chunk is currently being read. This helps you keep track of your progress through long articles.

Iframe content is automatically detected and included in the text extraction, so you don't miss any content that might be embedded in the page. This is particularly useful for articles that use iframes to display additional content.
